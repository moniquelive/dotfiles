# Dev.to Blog Style

- Start from the concrete problem or annoyance that motivated the work.
- Keep the article practical and implementation-driven.
- Prefer short sections, examples, commands, and small explanations.
- Use a casual first-person voice without over-polishing.
- Include code/config snippets when they clarify usage.
- Explain reliability or design choices only when they mattered.
- End with a short closing section: what worked, what might come next.
